What is Kana Kombucha?

Kana Kombucha is a small-batch, artisan kombucha brewed in Lithuania. We believe in the power of fermentation — a tradition thousands of years old — to create delicious, living beverages that nourish from the inside out.

Every bottle is brewed with organic tea, pure cane sugar, and a living SCOBY culture. We flavour our kombucha with real fruits, herbs, and botanicals — never artificial flavourings or concentrates.
